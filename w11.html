5- What do you do, steps or processes related to this week's topic?

Leadership is done in processes because of the many skills required to be an effective leader. Leadership includes the process of getting to know a team, and listening and directing them in order to achieve a collective goal. There is no right or wrong way to do any of these, but they have to be worked on together to become effective- more than one thing at a time.

6- What is the most significant takeaway you have gained from your study this week?

"Our deepest fear is not that we are inadequate. Our deepest fear is that we are powerful beyond measure. It is our light, not our darkness that most frightens us." -Marianne Willamson.

This quote was very striking to me. I have many goals that I haven't put time toward because I fear failing- but what if I also fear the success? What will I do if I'm successful at them? Looking from this perspective, I am much more motivated to try, if things happen to turn out well. Success or failure, most experiences are worth it.
